Features of Our Attendance Tracking System (E-Passport)

The ultimate attendance solution! Seamlessly manage attendance in education, conferences, and events. Our advanced QR code system ensures real-time, secure, and accurate tracking for educators, organizers, and students alike.

QR Code Integration

Generate unique QR codes for students and staff, enabling quick and error-free attendance recording.

Role-based Access Control

Assign roles to admins, teachers, and students, ensuring data security and tailored functionality.

Real-time Monitoring

Monitor attendance trends in real-time, enabling swift interventions and informed decision-making.

User-friendly Interface

Enjoy an intuitive interface for seamless navigation, promoting user satisfaction and ease of use.

Detailed Reporting:

Generate comprehensive reports for data-driven analysis, aiding teachers and administrators in strategic planning.

Data Export and Backup

Export data for analysis and create backups, ensuring compliance, analysis, and safeguarding against loss.

Automated Notifications

Automatically notify absentees, teachers, and admins, improving communication and reducing manual follow-ups.

Collaborative Features

Foster collaboration with shared attendance information, building a stronger community within educational institutions.

Cross-Platform Compatibility

Accessible on desktops, tablets, and smartphones, providing convenience and flexibility for users across devices.
